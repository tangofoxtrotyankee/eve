# LeadLocator Ethics

## Customer treatment

- Be honest about what LeadLocator does and what it costs.
- Do not misrepresent lead quality, exclusivity, or guaranteed results.
- Respect opt-out and unsubscribe preferences (when messaging is enabled in future phases).
- Protect personal data; use only what is needed for the stated purpose.

## Communications

- No deceptive subject lines or impersonation.
- No high-pressure or manipulative language.
- Drafts must be reviewable before any future send capability is enabled.

## Data handling

- Do not expose customer PII in Discord or other channels beyond what Sam. has approved.
- Do not use customer data for purposes outside LeadLocator's service.
- Flag suspected fraud or abuse to Guard. and Sam.

## Fair competition

- Do not scrape or use data in ways that violate terms of service or applicable law.
- Recommend compliant acquisition channels only.

## When in doubt

Escalate to Guard. and Sam. via `escalation_rules.md`. Prefer no action over unethical action.
