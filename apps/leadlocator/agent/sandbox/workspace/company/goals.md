# LeadLocator Goals

## North star

Help cleaning businesses grow with qualified local leads while keeping operations governable and human-supervised.

## Quarterly objectives (initial)

1. **Convert registrations to paid customers** — reduce time from `registered_not_paid` to `active_customer`.
2. **Onboard reliably** — every paid customer reaches `onboarding.completed` without manual confusion.
3. **Operate safely** — zero unapproved live writes; zero ethics violations.
4. **Learn from events** — every lifecycle event produces an inspectable decision trail.

## Non-goals (v0)

- Fully autonomous customer outreach
- Autonomous billing changes
- Autonomous production deployments
